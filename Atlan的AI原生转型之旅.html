Rethinking Leadership Hiring: Beyond "Smart People" 重新思考领导力招聘：超越“聪明人”

Conventional Wisdom: 传统智慧：

"Hire really smart people and let them go figure it out." This often sets leaders up for failure due to a lack of context. “招聘非常聪明的人，然后让他们自己去解决问题。”这通常会导致领导者因缺乏上下文而失败。

A leader's job is to make great decisions, which requires context. New hires lack years of company context. 领导者的职责是做出明智的决策，这需要上下文。新员工缺乏多年的公司上下文。

Pulpa's Approach: Pulpa的方法：

Be extremely involved for the first 3 to 6 months of a new leader's journey. 在新领导者入职的最初3到6个月内，要极度投入。

  • Decision Review: First 20 decisions are reviewed live with Pulpa.决策审查： 前20个决策与Pulpa进行现场审查。
  • Focus: How they think, why they think that way.重点： 他们如何思考，为何如此思考。
  • Goal: Prevent sub-optimal decisions by providing necessary context.目标： 通过提供必要的上下文，防止次优决策。
